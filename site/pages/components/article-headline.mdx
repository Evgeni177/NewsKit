## Props

<LegacyBlock marginTop="sizing040">
  <Prop name="children" type="string" required>
    The content of the article headline is passed as the child of the component.
  </Prop>
  <Prop name="kickerText" type="string">
    The optional kicker content, which appears before the headline content.
  </Prop>
  <Prop name="kickerColor" type="ColorKeys">
    The optional color of the article kicker. If passed without kickerText, this prop has no effect.
  </Prop>
</LegacyBlock>
