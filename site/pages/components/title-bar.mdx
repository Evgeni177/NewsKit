# Title Bar

## Overview

The title bar provides context and actions related to a particular section of content that follows below on the screen.

## Props

Title bar has a number of props to facilitate a variety of uses:

<LegacyBlock $marginTop="sizing05">
  <Prop name="children" type="string" required>
    The content of the title is passed as the child of the component.
  </Prop>
  <Prop name="headingComponent" type="React.ComponentType" default="Heading2">
    The heading tag used for the title. It should be one of the Heading Classes from fountations.
  </Prop>
  <Prop name="actionItem" type="React.ComponentType">
    An action item, most commonly a button, related to the title.
  </Prop>
  <Prop name="$titleAlignment" type="TitleAlignment" enum={TitleAlignment} default={TitleAlignment.Left}>
    The alignment of the title.
  </Prop>
  <Prop name="$containerBorder" type="TitleBarBorder" enum={TitleBarBorder} default={TitleBarBorder.None}>
    Places a border on the container.
  </Prop>
  <Prop name="$paddingLeft" type="boolean" default={false}>
    Left padding of the title according to main container.
  </Prop>
  <Prop name="$paddingRight" type="boolean" default={false}>
    Right padding of the title and button according to main container.
  </Prop>

</LegacyBlock>

## Anatomy

The recommended placement of items in a Title Bar is:

- Place a title on the left or center
- Place contextual actions to the right

![Exploded isometric view](image.url 'Exploded isometric view')

1. Surface
2. Title
3. Action items (optional)

### Container

The container takes up the full width of the grid area that's specified for example 12 column, 6 column etc.
![Images here](image.url 'Images here')

A border can be applied to the surface (a combination of top, bottom, top and bottom).

![Showing border](image.url 'Showing border')

### Title

The title should provide context for the content that sits below the title bar.

![Do - If title text is long, wrap the title to two lines.](image.url 'Do - If title text is long, wrap the title to two lines.')
![Don’t truncate text, hiding the full title may cause misunderstanding.](image.url 'Don’t truncate text, hiding the full title may cause misunderstanding.')
![Don’t shrink text to fit on a single line.](image.url 'Don’t shrink text to fit on a single line.')

The title can be aligned to the left or the center.

![Left title](image.url 'Left title')
![Center title](image.url 'Center title')

### Action Items

Action items can include a button or a link. They are positioned to the right of the container and they vertically align to the position of the title.

![Example with buttons](image.url 'Example with buttons')
![Don’t change the vertical position of the actions](image.url 'Don’t change the vertical position of the actions')

## Behavior

### Action hidden at smaller breakpoints

By default the action item is hidden on small breakpoints (xs and sm). This is to allow the action to then be placed at the bottom of mobile list for a better UX.

## Accessibility

Your title should be 3:1 colour ratio against a the background colour to be AA WCAG compliant.

![Don't put x colour on x colour](image.url "Don't put x colour on x colour")
