## Props

Divider have a number of props to facilitate a variety of uses. Custom and required props are detailed below:

<LegacyBlock marginTop="sizing05">
  <Prop name="color" type="ColorKeys">
    If provided, overrides the color of the divider.
  </Prop>
  <Prop name="border" type="BorderKeys">
    If provided, overrides the thickness of the divider.
  </Prop>
  <Prop name="marginTop" type="SizingKeys">
    Specifies the top offset of the divider.
  </Prop>
  <Prop name="marginRight" type="SizingKeys">
    Specifies the right offset of the divider.
  </Prop>
  <Prop name="marginBottom" type="SizingKeys">
    Specifies the bottom offset of the divider.
  </Prop>
  <Prop name="marginLeft" type="SizingKeys">
    Specifies the left offset of the divider.
  </Prop>
</LegacyBlock>
